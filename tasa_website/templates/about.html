About

Why TASA?

Home. Family. Memories. When we ask people to describe TASA, these are the words that never fail to show up in the answers. Our members come from vastly different backgrounds, and TASA is a space for us to embrace and celebrate all those unique experiences, while connecting through our shared passion for Taiwanese American heritage and culture. The Taiwanese-American identity is a complex oneâ€”not definable by any one set of criteria, but a combination of the diverse experiences of all Taiwanese-Americans. It is an ever-changing identity, which is what makes our work at TASA so rewarding and so exciting to do.

Our Mission

The Taiwanese American Student Association at the University of California, Berkeley is dedicated to promoting the unity and cooperation of Taiwanese-Americans in the community, the presence of the Taiwanese American community in local and national arenas, and the culture and identity of Taiwanese-Americans. TASA will hold events bringing together people of all backgrounds and ideals, uniting them by a common culture and interest, and together building the awareness of Taiwanese-Americans for their own identity and culture, knowledge in their heritage, and strong recognition from others.

Families

One of TASA's biggest draws is the family system! Families provide an opportunity to get to know other members on a more personal level in smaller and more frequent get-togethers. Some fun family events we've had in the past include picnics, water balloon fights, making dumplings, and just getting a bite to eat with your family! There are no rules about sticking only to one family, so feel free to attend any or all of our family events and find some lifelong friends!
