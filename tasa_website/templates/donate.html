How to Donate

Please make your checks payable to ASUC/TASA and mail to:

Taiwanese American Student Association
University of California
112 Hearst Gym, MC 4520
Berkeley, CA 94720-4520

You can also drop it off at the TASA table on Sproul Plaza during tabling hours, which are every weekday from 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM!
